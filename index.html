About This Space

A New Kind of
Game River Lodge

Crocodile Rock Lodge is a luxury self-catering safari lodge in Mjejane Private Game Reserve, overlooking the Crocodile River and Kruger National Park. Our exclusive-use Kruger accommodation sleeps up to 10 guests in 5 en-suite bedrooms — perfect for families and groups seeking authentic Big Five safari experiences. Wildlife moves freely between Mjejane and Kruger across their unfenced boundary — game viewing begins the moment you arrive.

Sleeping up to 10 guests (including children and infants, in line with reserve rules), the lodge comprises two bedrooms in the main house and three separate private cottages — offering both togetherness and personal space. For families with little ones, up to 2 stretcher beds can be set up in two bedrooms, each fully made up with mattress, linen and pillow.

Every detail has been considered — from a SMEG electric oven with SMEG gas hob, SMEG microwave, Samsung 90cm double fridge, full stand-up freezer and Philips large air fryer to Simon Baker cotton linens, a Speed Queen washer & dryer, Jetmaster wood braai, Chef 5-burner gas braai, and a Snomaster drinks fridge on the 19-metre verandah. Staff are happy to handle laundry on request. Self check-in via lockbox. Whole property for your exclusive use.
